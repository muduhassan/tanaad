## B) Functional Demo Runbook

1. Install the application on two PCs.
2. On the first run, select "Shop Device" on one PC and "Home Device" on the other.
3. Pair the devices using the QR code/token generated by the Home Device.
4. Log in as staff in both English and Somali.
5. Add one entry in each ledger pillar and confirm persistence in local storage.
6. Disconnect the network and add additional entries; verify that the queued count increases.
7. Reconnect the network and ensure that queued items sync and appear on the Home Device dashboard.
8. Trigger Milk and Bread alarms to test persistence and snooze functionality.
9. Respond to hourly checkpoints and log responses.
10. Simulate missing the closing checklist and verify the Red alert on the next startup.
11. Test Telegram functionality and verify that alerts work when the Home PC is OFF.
